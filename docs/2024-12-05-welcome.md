## What You Can Do Here

- **Write and publish blog posts** with full markdown support
- **Organize content** with tags and categories
- **Share knowledge** with a growing community
- **Create documentation** alongside your blog
- **Customize the platform** to match your brand

## Getting Started

To add your first blog post, create a new markdown file in the `blog/` directory with the following format:

```markdown
---
slug: my-post-slug
title: My Post Title
authors: [your-name]
tags: [tag1, tag2]
date: 2024-12-05
---

Your content here...
```
